## 1.1 What is  Prompt 
👉 A prompt is the instruction you give to AI.

🔥 But deeper truth:

👉 A prompt is how you PROGRAM AI using language

💡 KEY IDEA:
“The quality of your prompt = the quality of your result”

#### Example:
### Weak Prompt:

“Write about money”
👉 Output = boring, general 😴

### Strong Prompt:

“Act as a financial advisor. Explain how a university student in Kenya can start saving money with Ksh 500 per week. Give practical steps.”

👉 Output = specific, useful 🔥

## 1.2 Bad VS Good prompt

### Bad Prompts
•	Too short

•	No context

•	No direction

•	No structure

#### Example:
“Tell me about business”
👉 AI doesn’t know:

• What type?

•	For who?

•	Level?

### GOOD PROMPTS 
They include:

•	Clear goal

•	Context

•	Specific instructions

#### Example:
“Explain 3 small business ideas a beginner in Mombasa can start with less than Ksh 5,000. Keep it simple and practical.”

##  1.3 The Prompt Formula 
FORMULA:

👉 Role + Task + Context + Output Format

🔹 1. ROLE (Who should AI act as?)
Examples:
•	Teacher

•	Business expert

•	Doctor

•	Developer

•	Marketer

🔹 2. TASK (What should it do?)

Examples:

•	Explain

•	Create

•	Analyze

•	Suggest

🔹 3. CONTEXT (Extra details)

Examples:

•	Location (Kenya)

•	Audience (beginner)

•	Budget

•	Goal

🔹 4. OUTPUT FORMAT (How should it respond?)

Examples:

•	List

•	Steps

•	Table

•	Short paragraph

####  🔥 FULL EXAMPLE:
👉 “Act as a business coach (Role).

Suggest 5 online businesses (Task)

for a student in Kenya with no capital (Context).

Give step-by-step explanation (Output).”
