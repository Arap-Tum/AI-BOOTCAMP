# 🔍 AI for Research: Learn Faster with Smart Sources

## 🎯 Lesson Goal
By the end of this session, you will be able to:
- Use AI to gather and organize information

- Ask smart questions from sources  

---

# 🧠 What is AI Research?

AI research means:
> Using AI to understand a topic faster by analyzing documents, videos, and sources.

Instead of reading everything manually, AI helps you:
- Summarize  
- Explain  
- Compare  
- Answer questions
